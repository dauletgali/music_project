About SPM Creative Company USA

SPM was born from the convergence of music, cinema and creative direction. We are a house of creation that treats art as language, purpose and presence. With work that has circulated through Brazil, Latin America, the United States, Europe and Asia, we bring a global perspective shaped on stage, in the studio and behind the camera.

Gabriel A. T. Arevalo, João Paulo Faria and Marco Antônio do Carmo Pontes combine musical direction, audiovisual craft and orchestral expertise in a single axis — crafting works with artistic depth, aesthetic clarity and disciplined delivery.

Every project begins with identity, narrative and intention. We merge sound, image, artistic direction and strategy to transform visions into something you can see, hear and recognize.

How we approach each project

We listen before we design. Context, timing, audience and intention define the path.

From there, we build the creative and technical architecture: narrative, visual language, sonic identity, rhythm and structure. Only then we move into execution — with clear steps, aligned expectations and defined milestones.

The result is not just a piece of content or a song, but a coherent presence that can live across platforms, formats and stages.

Our pillars

Four fronts that interact constantly: cinema and image, music and sound, creative direction and strategy, and visual presence. Each one can live alone — but they gain strength when they move together.

Cinema & Image

Audiovisual production with language, narrative and direction. From institutional films to campaigns, music videos, live sessions, documentaries, short, mid-length and feature projects.

Music & Sound

Complete music production, original scores, sound design, orchestral arrangements, recording direction and sonic identity for artists, films and brands.

Creative Direction & Strategy

Identity building, artistic positioning, narrative development and creative planning for careers, brands and long-term projects.

Portrait & Visual Presence

Portraiture, institutional and editorial imagery, stills and visual branding with intentional composition and aesthetic discipline.
